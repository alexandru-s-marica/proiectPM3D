# Walschaert’s Valve Gear Assembly

This project focuses on designing and fabricating a functional, 3D-printable model of the Walschaert’s valve gear, a pivotal mechanical component used in steam locomotives to control steam distribution.

## 🎯 Project Goals
* **Mechanical Fidelity:** Design a functional assembly that accurately represents the complex linkages of the gear. The reverser handle will impact the "flow" of air into the pistons.
* **Manufacturing Optimization:** Ensure all components are optimized for 3D printing, considering tolerances and structural integrity.
* **Kinematic Demonstration:** Provide a clear visual and mechanical demonstration of the motion inherent in steam locomotive valve systems.
* **Final aim:** Adapt the pistons to use compressed air as a driving force, allowing the visualisations of the actual movement and mechanisms employed by a real steam locomotive.

## 🛠 Tools Used
* **CAD Software:** Autodesk Fusion 360
* **Hardware:** 3D Printer
* **Materials:** PETG

## ⚙️ Components Included
The assembly consists of several key mechanical parts:
1.  **Eccentric Crank and Rod:** Drives the expansion link.
2.  **Expansion Link (Link Motion):** Allows for reversing and variable cutoff.
3.  **Radius Bar:** Connects the expansion link to the combination lever.
4.  **Combination Lever:** Combines motion from the crosshead and the radius bar.
5.  **Union Link:** Connects the crosshead to the combination lever.
6.  **Valve Stem:** Transmits final motion to the piston valve.
